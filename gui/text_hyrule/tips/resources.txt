RESOURCES
Resources are divided between Food, Material, Ore, and Currency, though some Factions exploit unique resources or methods.
Fruit and Meat, Wood, Stone, Metal, and Rupees are the most commonly found types of Food, Material, Ore, and Currency.
Be sure to protect your Workers! If an enemy disrupts your economy by attacking Resource Gatherers, it can quickly lead to defeat.
The type of Map can determine the abundance or scarcity of some Resources, so become familiar with different environments!
